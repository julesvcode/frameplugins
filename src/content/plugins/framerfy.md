## Overview
Framerfy is a powerful no-code plugin designed to turn your Framer projects into fully functional Shopify storefronts. By combining the world-class design capabilities of Framer with the robust e-commerce backend of Shopify, Framerfy enables designers and entrepreneurs to build unique, high-performance shopping experiences without writing a single line of code.

## Key Features
- **Live Shopify Sync:** Connect your store via the Shopify API to automatically import products, variants, images, and pricing.
- **Customizable Commerce Components:** Access a library of pre-built, flexible components for product grids, detail pages, and shopping carts.
- **Headless Performance:** Benefit from the speed of a headless architecture while maintaining the ease of use of a visual builder.
- **Dynamic Cart System:** Includes a fully functional cart overlay that syncs in real-time with Shopify’s checkout system.
- **Inventory Management:** Ensure your site always displays the most accurate stock levels directly from your Shopify admin.

## How to Use Framerfy
1. **Connect Shopify:** Install the plugin and enter your Shopify Store URL and API Access Token.
2. **Import Products:** Use the visual interface to choose which products or collections to sync into your Framer project.
3. **Design Layouts:** Drag and drop Framerfy components onto your canvas and style them using Framer’s native tools.
4. **Setup Checkout:** Configure the checkout redirect to ensure a seamless transition for customers to the Shopify checkout page.
5. **Go Live:** Publish your site directly and start accepting orders instantly.

## Use Cases & Examples
- **Boutique Fashion Stores:** Designing highly interactive product pages with smooth animations that traditional Shopify themes can't replicate.
- **Single Product Launches:** Creating high-conversion landing pages for new product drops with integrated "Buy Now" functionality.
- **Global Storefronts:** Leveraging Shopify's multi-currency and multi-language support while maintaining a custom design in Framer.
